## 1. Pavel Durov's Personal Telegram Channel

Durov's primary communication channel is his personal Telegram channel, where he posts major announcements, philosophical reflections, and product updates. Key posts:

### 1.1 Platform & Product Announcements

**Mini App Store & Web3 Browser (July 2024)**
- Announced Telegram would launch a dedicated Mini App store and Web3 browser
- "2024 will go down in history as the year when hundreds of millions of people became familiar with blockchain"
- "Telegram is at the epicenter of this societal transformation"

**Hamster Kombat Growth (July 2024)**
- Described Hamster Kombat as "the fastest-growing digital service globally"
- Noted it was onboarding 4-5 million new players daily
- 239 million users in 81 days

**Ad Revenue Sharing (February 2024)**
- Announced 50:50 ad revenue split with channel owners via TON Blockchain
- "Online ads should no longer be synonymous with abusing user privacy"
- "If Telegram starts earning money, the community should also benefit"
- Payouts exclusively in Toncoin, initially in nearly 100 countries

**Gift Marketplace Launch (May 2025)**
- Announced in-app Gift Marketplace
- Reported "users have acquired more than 20 million Gifts" on Telegram
- Positioned gifts as "socially relevant NFTs" in billion-user ecosystem

**Telegram Stars Currency (2024)**
- Introduced Stars as in-app virtual currency for digital goods/services
- Seamless purchases via Apple Pay/Google Pay
- Developers can swap Stars for Toncoin or discounted Telegram ads

### 1.2 Philosophy & Values Posts

**On Advertising Philosophy**
- "Displaying ads in private one-to-one chats or group chats is a bad idea"
- "Communication between people should be free of advertising of any sort"
- Ads only in public channels with 1000+ followers, limited to 160 characters
- "We came up with ways to monetize Telegram without abusing people's personal data"

**On Freedom & Privacy**
- Consistent messaging: Telegram as "a neutral platform that is impartial and doesn't take any side"
- "Respectful to people's privacy and freedoms"
- Would "rather quit a market" than compromise encryption

**On Blockchain & Freedom**
- "We believe in blockchain, we think it's one of the technologies that enable freedom"
- "I think this is the way money should work. Nobody can confiscate your Bitcoin from you. Nobody can censor you for political reasons"

## 2. Official Telegram Blog (telegram.org/blog)

### Key Product Milestones
- **June 2015**: Bot API launch — open, free API for developers to build bots
- **2017**: Bot Payments API — enabling commerce through bots
- **April 2022**: Telegram Premium subscription launch
- **2023**: Mini Apps platform expansion (formerly Web Apps)
- **2024**: Mini App Bar, Paid Media, Stars currency
- **April 2025**: Bot API 9.0 — secure/persistent local storage for Mini Apps
- **March 2025**: Telegram crosses 1 billion monthly active users

### Platform Design Philosophy
- Bot API has always been free and open
- No app store review process (unlike Apple/Google)
- Mini Apps use standard web technologies (HTML5, JavaScript)
- Since early 2025: all blockchain Mini Apps required to operate on TON
