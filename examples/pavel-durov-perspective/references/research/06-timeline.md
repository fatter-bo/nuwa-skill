## Key Life Phases

### Phase 1: Russian Prodigy (2006-2014)
- Built Russia's largest social network from university
- Became billionaire by age 27
- Developed libertarian convictions through conflict with Kremlin
- Sacrificed everything (company, country) for principles

### Phase 2: Exile & Telegram Building (2014-2021)
- Stateless entrepreneur; multiple citizenships
- Self-funded Telegram from Bitcoin holdings
- Built team of 30 elite engineers in Dubai
- Failed TON ICO taught regulatory lessons

### Phase 3: Platform Economy (2022-2024)
- Monetization: Premium → Ads → Stars → TON integration
- Mini Apps transform Telegram from messenger to super-app
- Crypto gaming explosion validates platform play
- France arrest makes him global symbol of encryption debate

### Phase 4: AI & Institutional Era (2025-present)
- Cocoon decentralized AI network
- Institutional investment (BlackRock, Mubadala)
- 1B+ MAU; mainstream recognition
- Balance between independence and institutional capital
