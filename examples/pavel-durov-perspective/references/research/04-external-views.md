## 5. Competitive Landscape Analysis

### Telegram vs. WhatsApp/Meta
- WhatsApp: 2B+ users, end-to-end encrypted by default, but closed ecosystem, no bot API comparable to Telegram's
- Telegram: 1B+ users, open Bot API, Mini App platform, TON integration — more open but encryption not default for all chats
- Key difference: Telegram's openness enables third-party innovation; WhatsApp's closedness provides more consistent privacy

### Telegram vs. WeChat
- WeChat: dominant Chinese super-app with Mini Programs (predecessor concept)
- Telegram Mini Apps: similar concept but with blockchain integration and global reach
- Key difference: WeChat operates under Chinese government oversight; Telegram positions itself as anti-surveillance

### Telegram vs. Signal
- Signal: gold standard for encrypted messaging, non-profit
- Telegram: broader feature set but Secret Chats (E2E) are opt-in, not default
- Signal criticized Telegram's encryption claims as misleading since standard chats are server-side encrypted only
