## 1. Tucker Carlson Interview #1 (April 2024, Dubai)

**Context**: First on-camera interview in almost a decade. Conducted at Telegram offices in Dubai.

### Key Statements

**On Leaving Russia (VKontakte)**
- Refused to ban opposition communities on VK when Russian government demanded it
- "I would rather resign and leave the country where I was born than participate in something like that"
- Refused FSB request to hand over Ukrainian protesters' personal data during Euromaidan 2013-2014
- Was fired from VK and forced to leave Russia

**On FBI/US Government Pressure**
- Experienced FBI airport interrogations upon arriving in the US
- FBI agents visited his home
- Alleged attempts to recruit Telegram engineers to introduce backdoors into code
- After January 6th: received contradictory demands from both Democratic and Republican congressional members

**On Telegram's Neutrality**
- "A neutral platform that is impartial and doesn't take any side"
- Applied content rules "equally to all sides" without prejudice
- Contrasted with Facebook which "tips the scale" in favor of certain movements
- During COVID: "one of the few or maybe the only major social media platform that didn't take down accounts" skeptical of pandemic measures

**On Personal Philosophy & Wealth**
- "I have a few hundred million dollars...and I don't do anything with it"
- Owns no real estate, jets, or yachts
- Prioritizes "freedom" above material possessions
- Would "rather make decisions influencing how a billion people communicate"

**On Business Model & Team**
- 100% ownership; avoided venture capital for independence
- Zero dollars spent on marketing; growth "purely organic"
- Only ~30 engineers; no HR department
- Selection through engineering competitions
- Operates "like a Navy SEAL team"

**On Encryption**
- Brother Nikolai designed encryption standards still in use with "minor changes"
- Assumes "devices I use are compromised" by default
- Governments describe "spying as security"

**On Competition**
- Competitors "copying what we did six years ago" with inferior quality
- Most platforms "would rather copy proven models" than innovate

## 2. Tucker Carlson Interview #2 (June 2025)

**Context**: First interview after arrest in France. Durov speaks out publicly about detention.

### Key Statements

**On France Arrest Experience**
- Spent "four days in police custody"
- Held in "70 square feet room, no windows" with minimal accommodations
- Found it remarkable that French authorities arrested him rather than using standard diplomatic channels
- Noted French consulate was "just two floors below Telegram's Dubai office"

**On Backdoors and Encryption**
- "If you implement the backdoor technically, other actors are able to exploit it"
- Banning encryption endangers law-abiding citizens while criminals switch to alternative apps
- Confirmed complying with legal court orders for identification data (IP addresses)
- Drew principled line: rejected demands for private message access

**On Telegram's Profitability**
- Telegram crossed 1 billion monthly users
- Became profitable through voluntary subscription services, not data exploitation

## 3. Lex Fridman Podcast #482 (October 1, 2025)

**Context**: 4 hours 43 minutes. One of the deepest public interviews Durov has given. Titled "Telegram, Freedom, Censorship, Money, Power & Human Nature."

### Key Statements

**On Team & Engineering**
- ~40 core engineers managing nearly 100,000 distributed servers
- Small team forces automation and efficiency
- "If you have too many people, they have to coordinate their efforts, constantly communicate, and 90% of their time will be spent on coordinating"

**On Telegram Gifts as NFTs**
- "We didn't like the old school NFTs. First of all, they were not relevant socially because, okay, you have an NFT, where do you demonstrate it?"
- "At Telegram, a telegram gift is there next to your name"
- Gifts are "a reinvented socially relevant NFT integrated into a billion-user ecosystem, but at the same time available on chain, transferable, which you can own directly, also based on TON"
- "It's part of your digital identity on Telegram"

**On Blockchain Philosophy**
- "We believe in blockchain, we think it's one of the technologies that enable freedom"
- Bitcoin kept him financially stable; Telegram only became profitable in 2024
- "I think this is the way money should work. Nobody can confiscate your Bitcoin from you"

**On Encryption Stance**
- "Telegram has never shared a single private message with anyone, including governments"
- System designed so employees cannot access encrypted data even if compromised
- "If you don't like encryption, you don't like privacy...we'll just quit this market"

**On Digital Identity & Ownership**
- Telegram is "the first app that allows users to directly own their digital identity and username via NFTs and smart contracts"

## 4. Blockchain Life 2025 (Dubai, October 2025)

**Context**: Keynote speech at 15th edition, 16,730 attendees from 130 countries.

### Key Announcements

**Cocoon Network Launch**
- Introduced Cocoon (Confidential Compute Open Network)
- L2 network on TON blockchain for decentralized AI
- GPU owners rewarded with TON tokens for processing AI inference
- Telegram as first major client and significant investor

**Developer Platform for Mini Apps + AI**
- New platform launching November 2025
- Tools for creating Telegram Mini Apps with AI capabilities
- Companies and independent developers can launch services within messenger

**Freedom & Values**
- Telegram remains symbol of fight for "freedom of speech" and "the right of people to control their own data"
- "Freedom, dignity, and independence are the highest values that we must preserve for our children"
