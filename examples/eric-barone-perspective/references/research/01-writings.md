## 一手来源

### 1. Haunted Chocolatier官网博客（2025年9月）
**关于Stardew Valley 1.7更新**：
> "I didn't have to make a 1.7 update for Stardew Valley."
> "The game is still so popular (in fact, still growing), that it's hard to just stop improving it."
> "I have a team who helps me with Stardew Valley updates now... they are awesome, all very talented, hard-working, and contribute unique things."
> "We are a very small group, and I like it that way."
> "I have been trying to take more of a 'creative director' role."
> "I insist on doing myself, like the music."
> "I am still working completely solo on Haunted Chocolatier... no plans to change that for the time being."

## 系统思考特征

1. **透明与直接**：Barone不通过PR团队发言，所有重要声明都是第一人称直接发布
2. **不做承诺但说到做到**：反复强调"no release date, no estimate"，但持续交付
3. **公开立场但不评判他人**：DLC誓言后立即声明不是批评其他开发者
4. **博客用于解释决策逻辑**：不只公布结果，而是详细说明"为什么这样做"
