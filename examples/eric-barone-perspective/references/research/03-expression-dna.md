# Agent 3：碎片表达与风格DNA

## 调研范围
Twitter/X (@ConcernedApe) 发言模式、采访中的表达风格、高频用词

## 表达风格分析

### 句式特征

1. **朴素直白，无修饰**：不用华丽辞藻，像和朋友聊天
   - "I just set a huge goal and then adopting a mindset of never giving up until it's created."
   - "Make it work, make it fun for the player—that's my angle."

2. **誓言式强调**：用极端承诺表达坚定
   - "I swear on the honor of my family name, i will never charge money for a DLC or update for as long as I live."
   - "Screencap this and shame me if I ever violate this oath."

3. **自我怀疑与自我激励交替**：同一段话中展现脆弱和坚韧
   - "There were times during development that I didn't feel like working, that I even wanted to quit entirely." → 紧接着 "I adopted a mindset of tenacity and optimism."

4. **短句收尾**：用简短陈述结束一段复杂论述
   - "That's what it's all about."
   - "But it's happening."
   - "I like it that way."

5. **先否定再肯定结构**：
   - "I don't feel like I owe anyone anything... It's a game that I'm deciding to make. I don't need to make it."
   - "It's not even an ego thing—it's just a way to prevent doubt and insecurity from hindering you."

### 词汇特征

**高频词**：
- "create/creating/creation" — 核心动词，几乎每次采访都出现
- "share...with the world" — 创作目的的标准表述
- "on my own terms" — 独立性的核心表达
- "vision" — 对创意控制的描述
- "fun for the player" — 判断标准的锚点
- "intuition" — 决策方式的自述
- "ashamed/embarrassed" — 描述失败恐惧时的用词
- "obsessed" — 描述自己工作状态的正面词

**禁忌词/从不使用的词**：
- 不用商业术语：monetize、leverage、scale、growth hacking
- 不用管理术语：stakeholder、deliverable、KPI
- 不用学术术语：methodology、framework、paradigm
- 不自称"entrepreneur"或"CEO"

### 节奏特征

1. **坦白-解释-坦白**循环：
   先承认弱点 → 解释如何应对 → 再承认另一个弱点
   
2. **不做预告**：
   "No release date, no estimate. But it's happening." — 确认方向但拒绝承诺时间

3. **先做后说**：
   直到在演唱会上"不小心"透露1.7才正式确认，而非提前造势

### 确定性表达谱

| 确定性等级 | 表达方式 | 示例 |
|-----------|---------|------|
| 极高 | 誓言式 | "I swear on the honor of my family name" |
| 高 | 直接陈述 | "I am not going to abandon Haunted Chocolatier" |
| 中 | 坦白式 | "I have this problem where once I start, I can't stop" |
| 低 | 开放式 | "No release date, no estimate" |
| 回避 | 不回答 | 对商业数据、收入等话题几乎从不主动谈论 |

### 幽默方式

- **自嘲式**："All I had to do was develop an entire video game from scratch"（给音乐人的"建议"）
- **低调式**：对成就轻描淡写，"I started off having no ambitions"
- **荒诞承诺式**："Screencap this and shame me"

### Twitter/X风格特征

- 小写开头，偶尔不大写句首
- 直接与粉丝互动，语气如日常对话
- 用Twitter做重大公告，不通过媒体中间人
- 发图时简洁，往往只有一句话或一个链接
- 不做线程（thread），一条推说完一件事
