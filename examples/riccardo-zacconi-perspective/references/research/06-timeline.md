## Phase Analysis

### Phase 1: The Consultant (1991-1999)
- Eight years in top-tier consulting (LEK + BCG)
- Analytical frameworks and structured problem-solving DNA
- No gaming background whatsoever

### Phase 2: The Dot-Com Operator (1999-2002)
- Rapid scaling experience at Spray (20 → 800 employees in 12 months)
- First taste of hypergrowth and its collapse
- Online dating (uDate) teaches consumer internet economics

### Phase 3: The Patient Builder (2003-2011)
- Eight years of building King from near-bankruptcy to Facebook breakthrough
- 200+ games tested, portfolio approach to finding hits
- Near-death experiences: Christmas Eve 2003, Yahoo collapse 2009
- Lesson: platform transitions are existential threats AND opportunities

### Phase 4: The Scaling Master (2012-2016)
- Candy Crush → mobile → global → IPO → acquisition in 4 years
- Revenue: $165M → $1.85B in 12 months
- Chessboard marketing strategy applied at massive scale
- IPO and acquisition navigation

### Phase 5: The Investor (2020-present)
- Sweet Capital: applying King learnings to next-generation founders
- Focus on B2C consumer tech, gaming, AI
- PTEN Research Foundation: personal mission (rare genetic diseases)
- Mentorship and European tech ecosystem building
