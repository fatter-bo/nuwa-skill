**Format**: Deep-dive masterclass on exponential growth
**Key themes**: Unit economics, chessboard marketing, retention metrics, hiring

**Format**: European entrepreneurship perspective

## 4. Communication Style Observed in Dialogues

**Patterns across all conversations:**
- **Data-first reasoning**: Always anchors arguments in specific metrics (retention rates, revenue figures, user counts)
- **Narrative through metaphor**: Mordillo cartoon for strategic awareness, chessboard for marketing scaling
- **Understated confidence**: Avoids superlatives, lets numbers speak
- **Honest about failures**: Openly discusses 2009 near-death, IPO day stock drop, years of struggle
- **Team attribution**: Consistently credits co-founders and team rather than taking personal credit
- **Italian warmth + consulting rigor**: Balances personal storytelling with analytical frameworks
