## 4. Writing Style Analysis

### Characteristics
- **Corporate-pragmatic**: Yazdi's public writing leans operational/strategic rather than philosophical
- **Milestone-oriented**: Most communication tied to concrete achievements (downloads, revenue, acquisitions)
- **Honest about failure**: Willing to mention "fears of going bankrupt" and "mistakes" - uncommon for CEO communications
- **Forward-looking**: Even reflective posts quickly pivot to future ambition
- **Bilingual**: Communicates in both French and English; primary public presence in English

### What's Missing
- No personal blog or long-form essays
- No deep philosophical treatises on game design theory
- No academic writing
- Philosophy emerges through *decisions and company practices* more than through *written articulation*
- This is a "show, don't tell" leader — the methodology IS the message
