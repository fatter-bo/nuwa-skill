# Alexandre Yazdi - Expression DNA Research

> Research date: 2026-04-12
> Sources: Cross-referenced from interviews, LinkedIn posts, press releases, and industry coverage

---

## 1. Communication Style Overview

Alexandre Yazdi is a **low-profile, operationally-focused CEO** whose communication style contrasts sharply with the flamboyant founders common in gaming and tech. He is not a thought-leader-as-personality type; his public presence is deliberate, measured, and tied to concrete outcomes.

### Key Characteristics

**Pragmatic over philosophical**: Yazdi doesn't theorize about game design in abstract terms. He talks about metrics, thresholds, processes, and results. The philosophy is embedded in the methodology.

**Honest about failure**: Unusual willingness to acknowledge mistakes, near-bankruptcy, and wrong turns. "Hard work, fun, mistakes, ups & downs, breakthroughs, failures, fears of going bankrupt" — this is not typical CEO messaging.

**Collective framing**: Consistently uses "we" over "I". Credits teams, studios, and the system rather than individual genius.

**Bilingual but English-primary**: French native speaker operating in global English. Communication is clear, direct, occasionally formal in the European business tradition.

**Data-anchored**: Nearly every strategic statement is accompanied by a metric. Revenue figures, retention percentages, download numbers — Yazdi speaks in numbers.

- "Relentlessly innovating" — drive without sentimentality
- "Tomorrow's iconic products" — ambition toward lasting impact, not just downloads
- "The quality of execution and our ambition" — pairs craft with scale
- "Redefine the future of digital entertainment" — positioning beyond gaming

## 3. Emotional Register

### What He Projects
- **Controlled ambition**: Big goals stated matter-of-factly
- **Operational confidence**: Certainty about process, not about outcomes
- **Measured vulnerability**: Acknowledges past failures but frames them as learning
- **Engineer's precision**: Metrics-first communication

### What He Avoids
- Hype language ("revolutionary", "disrupting")
- Personal branding / personality cult
- Theoretical abstraction without data backing
- Public criticism of competitors
- Price/valuation boasting (mentions revenue, not valuation)

## 6. Expression DNA Summary

Yazdi's expression is that of an **engineer-turned-CEO who lets the system speak for itself**. He built a methodology that IS the message — the CPI test, the kill-fast culture, the studio network. His personal expression is subordinate to the operational philosophy. This makes him unusual: **the brand is the process, not the person**.
