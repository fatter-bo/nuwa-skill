### IVP Hyper-Growth Podcast: 6 Lessons from Paananen
- URL: https://medium.com/parsa-vc/ivps-hyper-growth-podcast-with-ilkka-paananen-ceo-of-supercell-5cbde80b7e6
- 6 key lessons:
  1. Make decisions for long-term success (pivoted from browser to mobile)
  2. Empower small independent teams
  3. Hire people who prioritize company interests
  4. View failure as essential learning
  5. Preserve game soul while localizing details
  6. Maintain focus during hypergrowth
- Key quote: "In most companies, the vision is held by the leadership team, but we started to ask ourselves what if the vision was held by the game developers?"
- Key quote: "Failing isn't fun... We don't celebrate the failure, rather the learnings that come from those failures."
- Key quote: "Our approach is to stay true to what our game is about in its heart and soul but then to localize everything around it."
