# Research File 05: Biography & Career Timeline

## Source Type: Mixed (interviews, biographical profiles)

### Personal Background
- Born: June 16, 1978, in Piikkio, Finland (couple hours west of Helsinki)
- Parents: both teachers
- Brother: Jukka Paananen (later co-founded Nonstop Games)
- Education: Helsinki University of Technology, industrial engineering, master's degree (1998)
