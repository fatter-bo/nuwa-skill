### Key External Observations:
1. Supercell's model is often cited but rarely replicated - the culture requires extreme discipline
2. The Tencent acquisition was unusual in preserving operational independence
3. 7-year gap between Brawl Stars (2018) and next major launch raised questions about model sustainability
4. EUR 3B revenue with ~500 employees = extraordinary per-employee productivity
5. The "least powerful CEO" concept has influenced management thinking beyond gaming
6. Finnish cultural context (flat hierarchy, trust-based society) is seen as enabler
7. AI is viewed as potential amplifier of small-team model (Paananen: fewer people, more capability)

### Criticism / Challenges:
- Model may not scale beyond gaming
- Heavily dependent on founder's personality and Finnish cultural norms
- High kill rate can be demoralizing despite champagne tradition
- 7-year hit drought suggests model may need evolution
- Tencent ownership creates tension with "independence" narrative
